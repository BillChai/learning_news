10. **[AWS/CDK] CloudFormation 與 CDK 為所有 stack 操作加入部署前驗證（pre-deployment validation）**

    來源：AWS What's New（2026-03）— <https://aws.amazon.com/about-aws/whats-new/2026/03/aws-cdk-mixins/>
